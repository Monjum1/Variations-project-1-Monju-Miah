The Rest


Jane Hoffman


Still, I keep myself, I take to bed. One lung is red. Cut red flowers hung in pink water.
  • My other lung is out of line. From one lung, I tell the truth. From the other lung, I lie. pink flowers hung in red water.

    • Like a pain, the truth is mine. The lie is that today I want to die. Cut red water hung in pink flowers.
