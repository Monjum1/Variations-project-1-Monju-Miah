The Rest
Jane Hoffman

      Still, I keep myself, I take         to bed.




One lung is red. Cut red flowers hung in pink water.           My other lung is out of line.
 From one lung,


I tell the truth. From   the other lung, I lie. Cut pink flowers hung    in red water. Like                         a pain,


the truth is  mine. The lie is that   today I want to die. Cut    red water hung in pink flowers. The rest of it is   stillness, rest. A soft cough into a hard pan. A hard   cough into a soft plane.
